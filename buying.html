Process for Buying a Lot

Remote purchase (from your home country)

  1. Contact Ron to arrange 10% down payment and forward a signed offer to purchase. A sample can be downloaded below. We will send you an official contract upon verbal agreement.
  2. We will forward this to our lawyer on Roatan, Raul Sierra Barrientos, to begin the title transfer process. Or, you can send it by courier to Raul directly (contact details below).
  3. On completion of title registration and your payment of transfer taxes and legal fees you will also send the balance. Your title will be sent to you immediately by secure courier.

Local purchase (while on Roatan)

  1. contact Ron Griffith directly on Roatan. Email griffith_ron@hotmail.com or call (011) 505-8983-3278 to arrange meeting.

    See our Lawyer on Roatan (Raul Sierra Barrientos) to sign offer and begin process.
