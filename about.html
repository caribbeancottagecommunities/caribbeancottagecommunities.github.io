About Carribean Cottage Communities

We are developers of 'Boutique Communities' currently focused on The Bay Islands Of Honduras. This is a young but rapidly evolving market and opportunity for those who dream of and are seriously considering owning a home, getaway property, or sound investment with substantial appreciation potential. This extraordinarily beautiful part of the world still offers the opportunity for people of fairly ordinary means to own such a property within our model. We sell directly to you with no realty fees, inflated costs, maintenance or Homeowners Association fees. Our goal is to build small communities of individual home owners who can pursue their own dream, design and creative direction of home building in this remarkable tropical setting. We believe in protecting and sustaining the natural beauty of our sites and leaving a small footprint. We have developed trusted resources and local people to work with fairly to help you cost effectively achieve your goals and implement your dream. We respect them, this unique environment , and local culture and customs. We invite you to be a part of this community and bring your creativity, initiative and smiles if you choose to become a part of a very special new community.

About Ron
Ron Griffith is a full-time resident of Roatan, a U.S and Canadian citizen. Ron owned a Nursery and landscape business in Manitoba for 17 years, and later had a financial planning practice for several years. He has traveled extensively in Latin America, and first came to Honduras and The Bay Islands in 2001 ... and fell in love with its natural beauty and culture. After many years of research and building trusted resources and friendships, he has formed Caribbean Cottage Communities to allow others who may want to share this rewarding adventure and experience.

Please feel free to contact Ron to assist you in buying your lot and or building your home.
