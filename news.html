ROATAN AGAIN VOTED THE BEST TROPICAL ISLAND FOR RETIREMENT
International Living Magazine is the latest to highlight Roatan as the world's best tropical island destination for retirement. Roatan has a progressive new government and infrastructure projects. The main town has seen a complete makeover and a new full hospital located in the Mall complex in French Harbor and operated by Cemesa Hospital, one of the finest in the region. Tourism is growing significantly with flights to the island now every day and more competitive prices. The article mentions lots as low as 60K but we have beautiful view lots at a substantially lower price with our 'self sustainable' options and many other advantages.

ROATAN VOTED ONE OF EIGHT GREAT PLACES TO RETIRE
Kiplinger.com has voted Roatan one of the eight greatest places to retire abroad. They cite the great climate, convenient flights from U.S., access to health care, very low cost of living, English-speaking locals, established expat community and real estate bargains.
