More details:

Phase II lots are larger home-sites with a large ridge-top width of 90 to 100ft or more. They are on average 100ft or more in elevation above phase I lots with even more extensive views and stronger trade wind breezes. All Guava Ridge sites offer privacy, tranquility, security and natural cooling with no need for air-conditioning: a paradise climate where you can sleep out on the veranda or roof top terrace without mosquitoes or flies to disturb you. Imagine yourself at the end of a day at the beach, diving, or just exploring this beautiful island and having a cool drink on your terrace, watching the cruise ships sail past while you prepare a dinner of seafood fresh from the ocean with island fruits and vegetables. As the evening progresses, the moon rises over Osgoode Cay, lighting the ocean with a 'silver highway' and a billion brilliant stars emerge so close you feel like you could almost reach up and touch them. All the while, the trade wind builds, cooling the night and creating the perfect natural environment for sleeping out under the stars. You will think you are indeed ... in paradise.

All homes in Guava Ridge will have their own self contained power systems which will give homeowners substantial savings. Island power is now at over 40 cents (USD) per kWh, about 3.5 times the average US rate and has frequent outages, inconsistent voltage and other problems. Our hybrid wind-solar systems will pay for themselves fully in less than 3 years and deliver consistant, more cost effective, clean energy. The systems are designed specifically for each home based on projected energy needs for the client.

Purchase is simple and straightforward. 10% non-refundable deposit and balance on transfer of title (about 30-45 days). Closing costs, including a land transfer tax and legal fees, will be approximately 10% of purchase cost. We personally invite you to join our unique community.

Financing specials:

We offer a simple affordable formula to help you get started. 40% of lot price on signing contract with 3 equal payments of 20% of lot price over 3 years. We offer this at zero interest. There is no better financing deal on the island.

$1500 instant rebate coupon for wind-solar system - With any lot purchase, we will pay the first $1500 of your hybrid wind-solar system costs**. This is a special incentive, in addition to our very cost efficient construction of these systems.
