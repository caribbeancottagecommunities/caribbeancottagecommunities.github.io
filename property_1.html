More details:

Only four cottage lots remain available and are listed at $17,500 (Lot D) to $22,500 (Lot G). All others are pending or sold. Each lot is approximately 1/10th acre and offers uniquely beautiful building opportunities. You can build on a flat ridge top, at tree top level, catching the brisk year round cooling S.E. trade winds and extend your deck right into the tree tops to the east over a very steep ravine of dense tropical forest above a deep valley with a beautiful creek in the bottom. To the west, the property descends in gentle tiers to a walkway and access lane and then a small deep valley in pasture. There will be no neighbors on either side. Each lot rises above the next as they ascend the ridge affording each lot extraordinary views. This will be part of a community of approximately 30 home sites in total with private access road and well. A beautiful small public swimming beach lies just below the property and is mainly used by the children of the small local community of Watering Place / Cedar Gully. It is clean and bug free. Most local residents are English speaking but also speak Spanish, and of course the local English Patois. They are friendly and very laid back.

All homes in Guava Ridge will have their own self contained power systems which will give homeowners substantial savings. Island power is now at over 40 cents (USD) per kWh, about 3.5 times the average US rate and has frequent outages, inconsistent voltage and other problems. Our hybrid wind-solar systems will pay for themselves fully in less than 3 years and deliver consistant, more cost effective, clean energy. The systems are designed specifically for each home based on projected energy needs for the client.

Purchase is simple and straightforward. 10% non-refundable deposit and balance on transfer of title (about 30-45 days). Closing costs, including a land transfer tax and legal fees, will be approximately 10% of purchase price. We personally invite you to join our unique community.

Financing specials:

We offer a simple affordable formula to help you get started. 40% of lot price on signing contract with 3 equal payments of 20% of lot price over 3 years. We offer this at zero interest. There is no better financing deal on the island.
